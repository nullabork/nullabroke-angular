Built for SEC research

Everything you need to search, filter, and read SEC filings — without the noise.

Query Builder

Write SQL-like queries with dynamic parameters — dropdowns, text fields, and number inputs that compile into powerful searches.

19 Blueprint Queries

Start with pre-built queries for IPOs, earnings, insider trades, proxy statements, mergers, and more. Customize them to fit your workflow.

Save & Organize

Pin your favorite queries, rename and duplicate them, and pick up right where you left off with auto-save and last-used tracking.

Document Viewer

Read filings directly in the app. Navigate exhibits, proxy statements, and attachments with a built-in sidebar and metadata panel.

XBRL Data Viewer

View structured XBRL financial reports with tabbed navigation. Balance sheets, income statements, and cash flows parsed and formatted.

Works on Any Device

Fully responsive on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Dark-themed for extended use. Sign in instantly with Google or GitHub.

Dynamic Parameters

Embed dropdowns, text fields, number inputs, and tag selectors directly in your queries. Change inputs without rewriting SQL.

Real-Time EDGAR Data

Queries run directly against the SEC EDGAR full-text search API. Results reflect the latest filings as they are published.

Query Engine

Search filings your way

Write SQL-like queries to filter by form type, company, date, ticker, tags, and more. Embed dynamic parameters directly in your queries — dropdowns, text fields, and number inputs that compile into the final search.

  • ✓ SQL-like WHERE clause syntax
  • ✓ Dynamic parameters with 4 input types
  • ✓ Sort, limit, and combine conditions
Organization

Save and organize your queries

Save your queries and access them instantly from the sidebar. Pin your favorites to the top, duplicate and rename them, or start from one of 19 built-in blueprint queries for common SEC searches.

  • ✓ Pin, rename, duplicate, and delete
  • ✓ 19 pre-built blueprint queries
  • ✓ Auto-save with last-used tracking
Documents

Read filings without leaving the app

Click any filing to open it in the embedded document viewer. Navigate between all documents in a filing — proxy statements, exhibits, XBRL data — with a sidebar showing metadata and company info.

  • ✓ View HTM, HTML, TXT, and XML documents
  • ✓ Filing metadata and company info sidebar
  • ✓ Direct links to SEC.gov originals
